How to Use

Exercise

This section is made for your exercising to be much easier! It has a bar at the bottom showing your streak to keep you motivated. Clicking on your past activity would show you what you have done before. Clicking on “exercise now” will take you to a page asking for the exercise you want to perform, and when you click on one, you get an option to start. When you start, your timer begins so you can track your exercise time!

Self Care

This section is made for you to keep in track of your mental well being. There’s a small quote at the bottom to remind you this is NOT selfish at all! Clicking on “selfcare ideas” will take you to a page suggesting a couple ideas. Clicking on “breathing” (in case you feel anxious) will give you instructions on how to breathe (to calm down) and clicking start will start the timer. In case youre feeling really down, we added a helpline section so you can talk to someone.
